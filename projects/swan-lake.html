Summary

Developed a visitor traffic monitoring system for the Swan Lake Christmas Hill Nature Sanctuary, a local Victoria, B.C. non-profit facing ecological pressures from increased urbanization and foot traffic. The system tracks visitor counts and identifies most-frequented park spaces, enabling data-driven resource allocation by sanctuary staff.

Technical Design

The prototype is an Arduino based custom PCB designed using KiCad housed in a 3D printed housing. The data visualization includes a heat map showing different levels of visitor traffic throughout the sanctuary.
